JASON
NASH

Jason Nash is a comedian, actor, writer, director, and Vine star. Jason created the web series "Jason Nash is Married," a film version of which was released last year. Jason also created the web series "How to Be a Man" and "The Shaman," and co-hosted and produced the long-running “Guys With Feelings” podcast.

BRANDON
CALVILLO

Brandon Calvillo is a comedian, writer, actor, and Vine personality (with over 6 million followers and over 3 billion loops!). Brandon has appeared in the TV series "SHFTY: Super Happy Funtime, Yay!", and "Warzone" - "FML" will be Brandon's feature film debut.
